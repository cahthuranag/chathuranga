Chathuranga Basnayaka

I am a PhD student at National Research Tomsk Polytechnic University(TPU) in Tomsk, Russia and a Research Associate in the Centre for Telecommunications Research at Sri Lanka Technological Campus (SLTC).I am fortunate to be advised by Prof.Dushantha Nalin K. Jayakody in the Infocomm Lab at TPU.

I was born in Sri Lanka and completed my Bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Information Engineering (with honors) from the University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka, in 2018.Prior to joining SLTC in 2019, I worked at University of Sri Jayawardanapura, Sri Lanka and University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka.

Research

My research interests lie in the UAV communication, Age of Information , and Ultra-Reliable Low-Latency Communication. The primary goal of my research is to develop a ultra reliable low latency communication system for the unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) by integrating UAV communication system into the existing and future cellular networks while minimizing age of information of the system.
